# Offline Android AI Chat Apps (Open Source)

Quick summary of the best open-source apps for offline local chat:

---

## 1. MLC Chat
- Test, recommended.
- [Official Site](https://mlc.ai/mlc-chat/)
- [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mlc.ai.chat) or [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/mlc-ai/mlc-chat-app/releases)
- Supports GGUF/GGML models, text chat, and often voice (mic icon).
- Best performance and community support.

---

## 2. Ollama Android (Experimental)
- [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/jmorganca/ollama-android/releases)
- Simple UI, GGML model support.
- Text only (for now).

---

## 3. F-Droid & Other Open-Source AI Apps
- Browse [F-Droid AI Chat category](https://f-droid.org/en/packages/?q=chat%20ai) for additional choices (features and support vary).

---

**Tip:** MLC Chat is the most user-friendly and best maintained for voice and text. Try it first!
